How to Pick the Best Bitcoin Betting Site

There are a substantial range of metrics to take into account when choosing the best Bitcoin betting sites.  Below, we offer insight into the most important factors to consider before selecting a provider. 

Secure Crypto Payments

When funding a BTC betting account, players can easily securely deposit funds – the process is much the same as funding a crypto exchange account. 

It’s simply a case of transferring Bitcoin to the sportsbook wallet address and then waiting for the provider to credit the funds.  Crucially, this means that there is no requirement to share any sensitive financial information with the best cryptocurrency betting sites. 

This is in stark contrast to how traditional sportsbooks work, which require players to manually enter their debit/credit card details to make a deposit. 

Fast Deposits & Withdrawals

When it comes to payment speeds, the best crypto sports betting sites offer lightning-fast deposits and withdrawals. 

Regarding the former, leading providers in this space will credit the betting account after just one blockchain confirmation – which typically takes 10 minutes.  Withdrawals are even more impressive, as the best Bitcoin betting sites will instantly approve a cash-out request. 

This means that there is no requirement to wait 1-3 days for the payments team to authorize the withdrawal, as is often the case with traditional betting sites and sportsbooks that deal with fiat money. 

Crypto Bonuses and Promos

Another huge benefit of opting for a crypto betting site is that oftentimes, platforms in this space offer lucrative Bitcoin casino bonuses.  We found that the top BTC betting sites offer attractive bonuses for both new and existing players. 

Lucky Block, for example, is offering new players a 200% deposit match up to 5,000 EUR (or crypto/fiat equivalent) + 50 free spins.

Low Crypto Banking Fees

The best Bitcoin betting sites enable players to deposit and withdraw funds on a fee-free basis.  This means that while the provider will not impose any transaction fees, the only charge to consider is that of the Bitcoin blockchain.  

In many cases, mining fees will sit below the $1 level – irrespective of how much is being deposited or withdrawn into the crypto sports betting site. 

Fair Deposit & Withdrawal Limits

When researching the best crypto sportsbooks, we found that top providers offer fair deposit and withdrawal limits. On the one hand, this means that players can often get started by meeting a minimum deposit of just $5-10. 

When it comes to withdrawals, we found that the best Bitcoin sportsbooks do not impose any cash-out limits. 

Wide Range of Crypto Betting Markets

Another metric to look for when choosing the best crypto betting site is the number of sports markets on offer. 

We would expect to see thousands of markets across soccer, tennis, football, basketball, baseball, and more. Moreover, the Bitcoin sportsbook in this space offers pre-match and live betting odds. 

Good Betting Features & Resources

In addition to the above, consider choosing a Bitcoin sportsbook that offers notable betting features and resources. Regarding the latter, this should include useful information and statistics on key sporting events. This will enable the player to make informed sports betting decisions. 

Some of the best crypto betting sites in this space also offer an in-play cash-out feature. This enables the player to lock in their sports betting profits before the match has finished. 

Why Use Crypto for Sports Betting?

A poll by Morning Consult found that 47% of sports fans are familiar with cryptocurrency and 27% currently hold some. 

Making use of their tokens on Bitcoin gambling sites is understandable – they offer unique perks and benefits that traditional fiat casinos cannot rival. This includes:

Instant Withdrawals 

We briefly mentioned above that the best crypto sports betting sites offer unparalleled withdrawal speeds. In most cases, the player simply needs to request a withdrawal to their preferred wallet address and this will be processed by the Bitcoin betting site autonomously. 

This means that the player does not need to wait for the withdrawal to be manually processed by the provider.  At a traditional fiat sportsbook, the end-to-end withdrawal process can take up to seven working days – sometimes more. 

Anonymous Transactions 

Gambling on sports at a fiat betting site requires the player to open an account and provide some personal information and contact details, while with crypto you can play in anonymous betting sites. 

Not only that, but the player will also need to go through a KYC (Know Your Customer) process before being able to make a withdrawal. This means uploading documents such as a passport and bank statement. 

Sites that let players bet with crypto, on the other hand, typically support anonymous accounts. This means that the player simply needs to provide an email address to get started.  

More Favorable Odds

Crypto sports betting sites that specialize exclusively in digital currencies often have much lower overheads when compared to fiat-centric platforms. 

This is because crypto payments operate outside of the traditional financial system, so there is a lot less red tape.  As such, the best crypto sports betting sites can pass on these overhead savings to players in the form of enhanced odds.
