Gamstop is a free service that enables self-exclusion, allowing individuals to block access to all gambling sites licensed in Great Britain. Users register with personal details like their address and birthdate, and the exclusion becomes effective within 24 hours.

UK casino players who are perhaps worried about their gambling habits can also access other sites such as GambleAware - which has a quiz to look at your habits among other information and resources - and GamCare, which offers support and advice via a 24/7 helpline, live web chat, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and forums.

How does GamStop work?

Users can choose an exclusion period of 6 months, one year, or five years. This decision is binding and can only be reversed at the end of the chosen timeframe. After expiration, there's an option to extend or renew the exclusion. Gamstop also works in partnership with various charities and organizations to assist those struggling with gambling addiction.
