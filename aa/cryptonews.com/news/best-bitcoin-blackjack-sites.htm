How Does Bitcoin Blackjack Work?

Bitcoin blackjack online works just like blackjack at a casino. Players bet the desired amount – which must be at least the minimum for the table – and then get dealt 2 cards.

After seeing those cards, players can choose to hit (get another card) or stay. The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Players win if they stay at or under 21 and beat the dealer.

Types of Bitcoin Blackjack Games

Blackjack is one of the most popular casino games in history, and many variations on the original blackjack game have developed. We’ll take a closer look at some of the most popular Bitcoin blackjack games players will find at the casinos we reviewed.

Multihand Blackjack

Multihand blackjack is simply blackjack played with 2, 3, or 4 decks. Playing with more decks is important because there are more copies of each card in the game. So, just because 1 player has an ace doesn’t mean that other players or the dealer can’t also get aces. 

Single Deck Blackjack

Single deck blackjack is played with just a single deck. There’s a lot of strategy involved in this variation because players know what cards are left in the deck based on what’s showing on the table. So, players can figure out the likelihood that they’ll go over 21 if they choose to hit.

European Blackjack

European blackjack is pretty close to the standard blackjack game, but with a small twist. The dealer is not required to check whether they have blackjack after the initial deal. If the dealer has blackjack, that wins out over any player that has 21 but not from a blackjack.

Vegas Strip Blackjack

Vegas Strip blackjack is one of the most popular variants on this classic game. Players are allowed to double down on either of their cards from the initial deal and can split up to 4 hands. They can also split any 10-point cards even if they aren’t the same card, so there are more opportunities to split hands.

Live Bitcoin Blackjack

Live Bitcoin blackjack is one of the most popular ways to play blackjack online. Live tables have a human dealer on camera and players are flanked by other players from around the world. It’s very similar to playing blackjack in a Las Vegas casino.

Players can talk to one another through a chat function and speak directly to the dealer. It’s possible to see what cards other players have, which can influence a player’s decisions if there are only 1 or 2 decks in play.

Live Bitcoin blackjack games may also have higher betting limits, but this varies by casino.
